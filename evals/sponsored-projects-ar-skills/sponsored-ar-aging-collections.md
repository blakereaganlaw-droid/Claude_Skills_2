# Evals — sponsored-projects-ar-skills:sponsored-ar-aging-collections

## 1. Positive trigger (should load the skill)
> "Which sponsors should we chase first? Build the aging by sponsor for our grants AR, flag
> concentration risk, and give me a prioritized call list with what it means for next month's
> cash."

Expected: skill loads; rolls up to sponsor/contract grain; declares the aging basis and
as-of date; separates future (billed-not-due) from overdue; restricts to RECEIVABLE-class
accounts and excludes draw-based awards; runs the cross-reference scrub (unapplied receipts,
partials, credits, disputes) before the call list; ranks by amount × age × sponsor-risk;
hands expected collections timing to cash forecasting with disclosed probabilities.

## 2. Near-miss (should NOT load this skill)
> "What's our DSO trend over the last 8 quarters and how does collections effectiveness look
> by sponsor category?"

Expected: portfolio KPIs/trends —
`sponsored-projects-ar-skills:sponsored-ar-kpi-trends-forecast`. If this skill loads instead,
sharpen the detailed-aging/worklist framing.

## 3. Quality rubric
A good response:
- **Does the task:** sponsor × bucket aging with basis/date labeled, concentration and drift
  read from the heatmap, a scrubbed prioritized call list with next actions and owners, and
  the cash-flow handoff.
- **Teaches:** triage-at-the-action-grain, the two aging bases, why raw aging overstates the
  problem until cross-referenced, and amount × age × risk as expected cash per call.
- **Safe:** open balances net of applications, terms-relative overdue, draw-based awards
  excluded, disputes routed to their own workflow, no sponsor balances/contacts committed.
