# Evals — treasury-accounting-skills:audit-readiness-and-pbc

## 1. Positive trigger (should load the skill)
> "The year-end audit PBC list just landed — 90 items — and last year fieldwork blew through
> the deadline with endless follow-up requests. Get us organized and ready."

Expected: skill loads; sets up the single PBC tracker with owners/dates and one coordinator;
applies the standalone-workpaper standard; pre-audits the perennial areas (bank recs +
complete account inventory before confirmations, debt/covenant workpapers, cutoff testing,
IC matrix); briefs walkthrough owners; sample-service and finding-response discipline.

## 2. Near-miss (should NOT load this skill)
> "Design segregation-of-duties and dual-approval controls for our payment process."

Expected: control design — `cash-management-skills:cash-management-controls`. If this skill
loads, tighten the audit-response/PBC framing.

## 3. Quality rubric
A good response:
- **Does the task:** working tracker, workpapers meeting the standalone standard, perennial
  areas pre-audited, walkthrough briefings, and a finding-response format with owners/dates.
- **Teaches:** the auditor's evidence asymmetry (why confirmations/samples/walkthroughs work
  the way they do), self-identified vs auditor-identified economics, and readiness as the
  exhaust of a clean close.
- **Safe:** corrects factual auditor errors with evidence rather than defensiveness, never
  reverse-engineers estimate rationale, keeps findings and correspondence out of committed
  files.
