# Federal payment methods and analysis worksheets (reference)

Educational summary of public rules (§200.305, §200.346). The award document and agency
payment-system terms control.

## Contents

## Payment method comparison
| | LOC / PMS drawdown | Reimbursement | Advance |
|---|---|---|---|
| Trigger | Institution draws against reported expenditures | Bill after spending (SF-270 or equivalent) | Forecast immediate cash needs |
| Invoice in AR? | Usually none — draws, not invoices | Yes — conventional transactions | Not AR: federal cash held (liability-like) |
| Right aging metric | Expenditure-to-draw lag | Days from *proper* request (30-day payment standard) | Time between receipt and disbursement (minimize) |
| Main risk | Draw too slow (working capital) or too fast/over (§200.346 debt) | Improper request restarts the clock; questioned costs | Holding excess federal cash; interest remittance |
| Fusion signature | PPM costs + lump-sum receipts; thin/no Receivables trail | Full PPM → AutoInvoice → AR pipeline | Cash received ahead of costs |

## Expenditure-to-draw lag worksheet (per draw-based award)
| Line | Source |
|---|---|
| Cumulative allowable expenditures (ITD, as-of) | PPM cost / expenditure data |
| − Cumulative draws (ITD, as-of) | Draw schedule / PMS records |
| **= Undrawn balance** | |
| Age of undrawn balance | Days since the expenditures were incurred (weighted or oldest) |
| Draw cadence vs policy | Actual draw dates vs your stated cadence |
Portfolio view: total undrawn, distribution of lag days, awards with lag > policy (e.g. > 14
or > 30 days), trend over quarters. Cross-check the corridor's other side: any award where
draws exceed expenditures → immediate review (§200.346 exposure).

## Cash-application checklist for draws
- [ ] Draw schedule (award → amount → date) captured at draw time, not reconstructed
- [ ] Receipt matched to draw batch; allocated to awards per the schedule
- [ ] No draw receipts parked as unidentified/unapplied beyond the current cycle
- [ ] Reconciliation: PMS/LOC statement ↔ cash received ↔ allocation, monthly
- [ ] Advances (if any): disbursement tracked against the advance; excess-cash interest rules checked

## Audit-defense package per draw
- [ ] Expenditure detail behind the draw (system report: award, period, cost categories)
- [ ] Allowability screen evidence (unallowables excluded before the draw)
- [ ] SF-270 or agency-equivalent request as submitted
- [ ] Approval per internal controls (who reviewed, who drew)
- [ ] Tie-out: draw amount = supported expenditures − prior draws
Assembled at draw time; the Single Audit samples this exact chain.
