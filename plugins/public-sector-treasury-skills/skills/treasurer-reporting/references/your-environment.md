# Your treasury reporting environment (sanitized template)

Fill in your institution's reporting setup. Real balances, holdings, and draft packages are
data — keep them in `your-environment.private.md` (git-ignored). Commit structure only.

- **Audiences and cadences:** <e.g. board finance committee quarterly; CFO monthly;
  treasury ops weekly — and any statutory/bylaw requirement to report, with its citation>
- **Exhibit list:** <which of the five standard exhibits you produce, plus any local ones>
- **Definitions:** <what "cash" includes; which balances count as available vs restricted;
  the liquidity metric used (days cash / months of spend) and its target floor>
- **Data sources per exhibit:** <bank portal/statements, custodian, LGIP statements, GL,
  forecast model — and who owns each pull>
- **Compliance check linkage:** <who runs holdings-vs-policy, when, where evidence is filed>
- **Template and archive locations:** <where the package template and issued editions live>
- **Sign-off chain:** <preparer → reviewer → treasurer/CFO; deadlines relative to meetings>
- **Known seasonal events to annotate:** <tuition due dates, appropriation receipts, debt
  service dates, fiscal year-end>
- **Public-records considerations:** <whether the report is a public record; review rules>
